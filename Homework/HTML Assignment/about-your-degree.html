Information Technology

Why I Choose This Field:

I choose IT without hesitation when it came time for college. As previously, yet minorly discussed on my About Me page, I built my first computer at ten years old. I have always been fascinated by technology and it's amazing to witness the growth of it as I grow up.

My main goal for the future is to have a creative, yet stable, job in IT. I'd be happy to be any role that has to do with software development, programming, or system adminstration. If I could I'd love to develop my own business relative to IT. I've always had a passsion for starting my own thing and I interned at a small IT company which let me see what a small IT business looks like.

The thing I find most interesting about my field is that it is constantly evolving. There is always something new to learn everyday which creates a challenging environment and a desire to be the best. Along with that, I love that the IT field is so vast. There really is a million different directions to go with an IT degree.
